Target Audience

Middle-age people from the Idaho community. People from Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. They are median income/middle-class people interested in being up to date with weather conditions and local news.

Persona 1

Persona: Office worker
Photo:
Fictional name: John Nelson
Demographics:
  • 55 years old
  • Married
  • Father of 6 children
  • Grandfather of 3 children
  • Has a bachelor's in Accounting
Goals and tasks: He is interested in his family and what happens in his community. I looks at his phone often to check the weather and watch the news.
Environment: He can use the computer, but doesn't consider himself an expert. He likes to say he is 'old fashion'.

Persona 2

Persona: Stay home mom
Photo:
Fictional name: Karren Smith
Demographics:
  • 48 years old
  • Married
  • Mother of 4 children
  • Grandfather of 7 children
  • Associates
Goals and tasks: She is most interested in the well-being of her family. She checks the weather often to make sure her family can go out and visit each other.
Environment: She can use the computer, but it is a little hard for her. She feels more comfortable using her phone.

Scenarios

  • What are the current trafic conditions in the community?
  • Are there any important things going on in the community right now?
  • I am going to be able to visit my grandchildren this weekend?
  • What is happening with the last political issue?
  • Are we going to be able to have the party tonight?
